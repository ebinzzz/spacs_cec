About Us

SPACS is dedicated to empowering computer science students by nurturing their programming and managerial skills, fostering an environment of growth and innovation.

  • Hosts engaging computer programming contests to sharpen coding abilities.
  • Organizes insightful seminars on various IT topics, ensuring students stay abreast of technological advancements.
  • Encourages the development of leadership and project management skills through interactive events.

SPACS is an organization for computer Science students. SPACS Aims at encouraging the talented youths of the college by enhancing their programming and managerial skills. SPACS conducts different activities such as computer programming contest, Seminars on IT related subjects etc..

Frequently Asked Questions about SPACS

Learn more about SPACS and its initiatives to enhance computer science students' skills.

What is SPACS?

SPACS (Student Programming and Computer Society) is an organization at our college aimed at fostering the talents of computer science students through various activities.

What activities does SPACS organize?

SPACS organizes computer programming contests, seminars on IT-related subjects, workshops on programming languages, and other events to enhance students' programming and managerial skills.

How can I participate in SPACS activities?

To participate in SPACS activities, you can join as a member by registering through the official college channels or contacting the SPACS coordinators directly.

Who can join SPACS?

SPACS is open to all computer science students interested in improving their programming skills and gaining knowledge in IT-related fields.

What are the benefits of joining SPACS?

Joining SPACS provides opportunities to network with peers, enhance technical skills, participate in competitions, and gain insights from industry experts through seminars and workshops.
